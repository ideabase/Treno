About Treno

Treno Ristorante, an upscale Italian restaurant, will be located in the historical train depot overlooking the Cuyahoga River in Downtown Kent, Ohio. The depot is currently undergoing a transformative renovation of its space, and the restaurant will be opening this summer 2017.


Treno will lend a cultural charm where patrons will feel included and part of the dining experience. With a menu focused on Northern Italian cuisine, our dishes will be prepared in house from scratch with fresh, flavorful ingredients including locally sourced products as available. We invite you to enjoy a memorable evening out or delicious lunch featuring exquisitely-prepared authentic Italian fare complemented by a thoughtful wine list and full bar selection.
